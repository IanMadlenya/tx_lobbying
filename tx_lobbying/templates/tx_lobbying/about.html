About the data

The two main sources of data are:

  • The lists of registered lobbyists, by year: http://www.ethics.state.tx.us/dfs/loblists.htm
  • And the coversheets for the lobbyist activies reports (LA): http://www.ethics.state.tx.us/dfs/search_LOBBY.html

Names come from both sources of data, but only the coversheets from activity reports (Form LA) have detailed information about names like first/last.

The information for lobbying interests come from the registration forms (Form REG). This information is all entered by hand and will be very hard to use without scrubbing.

You can see known data issues at GitHub.

Stories from The Texas Tribune about lobbying: Tribpedia: Lobbying.
